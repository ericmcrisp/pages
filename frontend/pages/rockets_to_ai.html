From A Career in Rocket Science to AI Development and Research

August 1, 2025 Eric Crisp

My career in aerospace was fueled by a passion of learning. I grew up failing math and science, and realized if I could master these topics, or at least understand them well, I would feel more compelte, more intelligent. In college, I got involved with combustion, aerodynamcics, and engine testing because I felt that engines were really where a lot of physics came together. If I wanted to master the concepts in the world around me, the best place to do it is where they all intersect. This is what motivated me for almost 10 years, through college, research, graduate school and over four years in industry.
